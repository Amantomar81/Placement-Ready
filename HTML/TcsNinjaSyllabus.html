TCS Ninja Numerical Ability Syllabus

The topics on which the TCS Ninja Aptitude syllabus is based on are:


Number System

  • Divisibility
  • Decimal Fractions and Numbers

Mensuration

  • Geometry
  • Area, Perimeter and Shapes

Arithmetic Ability

  • Ages
  • Averages
  • Equations
  • Probability
  • Percentage
  • Profit and Loss
  • Time and Work
  • Clocks and Calendar
