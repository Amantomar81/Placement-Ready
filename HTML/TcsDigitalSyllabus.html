TCS Digital Advance Quants Syllabus

This section assesses the mathematical ability of the student, topics covered under this section are:

  • Number System

    • HCF & LCM and Number System

  • Mensuration

    • Geometry

  • Arithmetic Ability

    • Ages
    • Allegations and Mixtures
    • Averages
    • Clocks and Calendars
    • Equations
    • Percentages
    • Permutations and Combinations
    • Probability
    • Profit and Loss
    • Ratios and Proportion
    • Series and Progressions
    • Time,Speed and Distance
    • Time and Work

  • Elementary Statistics

    • Mean, Median, Mode, Standard Deviation, and Variance

  • Data Interpretation

    • Data Interpretation
    • Graphical Data Interpretation
    • Pie Charts
    • Tabular Data Interpretation

  • Simplifications & Approximations

    • Simple Arithmetic Operations
