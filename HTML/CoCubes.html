CoCubes Online Paper Pattern 2021

There are two cases—if your college is registered to CoCubes, they will ask you to attend the test, and based on the score you will get calls from different companies for interviews. Also, you can register for CoCubes individually from their website directly. In the third case when companies like Capgemini visit your campus then the test for that company is conducted by CoCubes and based on the score, Capgemini may/ may not select you for the campus interview round.


While the syllabus for each section is given but for most companies, you will only have to give Quants, Logical, English, and Computer Programming MCQ/Pseudo Code Test.Once the result for these sections is declared the company may ask you to give the Coding Test as well.
