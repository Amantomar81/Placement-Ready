Interviews cannot be excelled in 1 day. It’s a thorough process that need practice on daily basis. Once you clear Online Test/Aptitude Test/Coding Test, final round is always an interview.

Interview rounds are generally divided into 1-3 rounds depending upon the profile:-

Case 1:

  • Technical Round
  • HR Round

Case 2:

  • Technical Round
  • Managerial Round
  • HR Round

Case 3:

  • Technical + HR Round(for some companies)

In all types of interview recruiter follows the same ideology, they test:

  • How confidant are you?
  • In what ways you present the things that you have mentioned in your resume?

Always remember, your resume is your identity proof or ticket to the job. You should be honest about all the details mentioned in it. Recruiter always dig in the resume coming up with tricky questions, programs etc.

Interview Experience FAQs

Ques: In how many parts the Interview experience is divided?

Answer: The whole process is divided into 3 parts mostly:-

  • Technical
  • HR Interview
  • MR Interview

Few puzzle questions are also asked. You can check our Interview experience dashboard.

Ques: Why is it important to prepare for an interview?

Answer: Interview is the most important aspect of your recruitment process. A candidate has to appear confident, calm and mature while also being someone who is in touch with their skills. Therefore it is very important to prepare for your interview.

Ques: What should I expect in a technical interview?

Answer: Technical interview is conducted by IT companies like TCS and Wipro to check a candidate’s technical knowledge and skills. In this interview you can expect questions from programming concepts and programming languages like C, C++, Java, Python or any other programming language which is mentioned in either your resume or in job description. You can also expect technical interview coding questions as well as database management questions.
