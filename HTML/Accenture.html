Most Recent Accenture Placement Paper Overview

Accenture used to conduct its hiring drive with 3 assessment rounds in the Zonal Level Hiring Test. But with the upcoming drive Accenture have changed its pattern .


Accenture Placement Paper 2023 will now have only one written round, which will have 6 different Sections. The 6 Sections will consist of 90 Questions ( The Analysis of which is given below in the image ). You will have 90 minutes to solve all the questions. And after clearing Accenture Placement Paper 2023 with the required cut-off, you will be eligible for the Interview Round.


The Accenture eligibility criteria is that you should have 65% or 6.5 cgpa in your graduation with no active backlogs.


Accenture Placement Test is now divided into further parts:


  1. Cognitive Assessment
    • Critical Reasoning and Problem Solving
    • Verbal Ability
    • Abstract Reasoning
  2. Technical Assessment
    • Common Applications in MS Word
    • Pseudo Code
    • Network Security and Cloud
  3. Coding
  4. Communication Assessment
  5. Interview

  • Salary

    • Associates Software Role– Rs.4.50 LPA (Breakdown below)
      • Fixed Pay - Rs 3.83 LPA
      • Performance Bonus - Rs 32,500/- per annum
      • One Time joining Bonus - Rs 25,000/-
      • Additional Benefits of Gratitude and Insurance
    • Software Engineering Role– Rs. 6.50 LPA
        • Fixed Pay - Rs 5.41 Lakhs
        • Performance based Pay - Rs 82,500/- (in a performance cycle)
        • One Time Joining Bonus ~ Rs 26,500/-
        • Additional Benefits of Gratitude and Insurance

  • Free Transport to everyone (Not Available in TCS, CTS, Infosys, Wipro, etc)
  • Other Allowances – House Rent Allowance, Medical Allowance, Health Insurance (4Lakhs Cover)

Accenture Test is now divided into several rounds and Cognitive Assessment is the first round of the written test. Cognitive Assessment is also bifurcated into several rounds i.e:


  1. Critical Reasoning and Problem Solving
