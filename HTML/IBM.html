Learning Agility Assessments:-

It is a Learning Agility Test, this is a Psychometric Test. In this Test, there will 50 pairs of statements were given. There is nothing right and nothing wrong with these questions; you just have to select which statement you agree with strongly or slightly! Based on that they will determine your personality. Around 50% of the students were cut off in this round only.
