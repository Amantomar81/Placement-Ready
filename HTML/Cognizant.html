Cognizant Placement Test Pattern Overview:

The cognizant drive is divided into 2 section


  • GenC Aptitude Assessment
  • GenC Communication Assessment

GenC Aptitude Assessment

Following are the sections that are asked in the Cognizant GenC Placement Exam Round 1-


  • Quantitative Aptitude
  • Analytical and Logical Reasoning
  • Verbal
Cognizant Placement Test No. Of Questions Time Difficulty
Quants 25 35 mins Medium
Logical 35 45 mins High
English 20 20 mins Medium
Total 80 100 mins Medium

Note: After clearing Round 1 if you score good marks you will be eligible for Round 2 Test which is termed as GenC Communication Assessment and if you didn't score good marks then you will be disqualified from the exam.

Communication Assessment:

In Round 2 you will be provided with 3 sections:

Sections Time Allotted
Reading and Grammar 45 mins(shared)
Comprehension and listening 45 mins(shared)
Speaking 45 mins(shared)

Note:-
  1. Students who have qualified the Aptitude Assessment will only be able to give the Communication Assessment

  2. If you will clear Round 2 you will be eligible to give an Interview for GenC profile.

GenC Interview:

In this Interview Round you will be asked question on Technical, Cognitive Abilities and Right Attitude. If you manage to clear this round you will get Cognizant Offer Letter.


  • Salary During Probation - 4 Lpa
  • Salary After Probation- 4.5 Lpa

Cognizant Automata Fix Questions and Papers -

This year they have introduced a new section called Cognizant Automata Fix, where in they will give you a code that will have a minor error like. Error in if else condition like or for loop where in one equal to sign is missing etc.


  • The Number of Questions are 7
  • Duration and time given is 20 minutes

Questions can be asked on:


  1. Basic programming
  2. Control Structures
  3. Conditional statements
  4. Linear data structures
  5. Advanced data structures
